Who We Are

We are a group of individuals who love feeling free and want to be free. Thus, we seek to broaden our ideas. We seek to expand the limits of our imagination.

What We Do

We believe ideas are broadened through exposure and introspection. In light of this, we seek to provide tools for exposure and introspection. Currently, our main focus is on the Technological space. As such, Tech-R-Us is currently organized into two divisions:

  • The Awareness Division

    As a sub-group within Tech-R-Us, we seek to raise awareness of the local and global tech culture mainly through discussions with industry persons. Checkout our Youtube Channel by clicking this link

  • The Projects Hub

    As a sub-group within Tech-R-Us, we seek to solve problems by developing technological solutions in an effort to provide more tools to enhance our way of living. You are interacting with our first project right now, our community website!
